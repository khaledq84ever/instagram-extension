InstaGet

Reels, posts, and stories — saved in seconds.

Open any Instagram post, Reel, or carousel and InstaGet pulls the original video or image straight into your Downloads folder.

1
Pin InstaGet to your toolbar
Click the puzzle icon in the browser toolbar, then click the pin next to InstaGet.
2
Open any post or Reel
An in-page Download button appears. Videos save as MP4; carousels and posts save as JPG. All land in Downloads/InstaGet/.
3
Or paste a URL into the popup
Click the InstaGet icon, paste any Instagram link, and choose video, image, or MP3 audio.
